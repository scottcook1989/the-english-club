Welcome to The English Club

Where we are:

We are an English language institute based in the center of Graz, Austria
Located just 2 hours from Vienna.
Graz is a city that mixes an old city with new innovation.

What we do:

We mix the learning of the English language with learning a new skill.
Students can enjoy learning coding, performing arts, sport or Mixed-Media.
Our staff encourage and motivate the students to use English by only speaking English.
