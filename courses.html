Our Courses

English and coding:

In our English and coding course students have 3 hours of English in the morning.
After lunch, the students will learn to code using Scratch, HTML and CSS.
The students will get to create either a game, app or a webpage.

English and Performing Arts:

In our English and Performing Arts course, students have 3 hours of English in the morning.
After lunch, the students will learn about acting, singing, dancing and stage presence.
At the end of the week, the students will put on a show they created themselves.

English and Sport

In our English and Sport course, students have 3 hours of English in the morning.
After lunch, the students will play a variety of sports from tennis, football and fitness.
At the end of the week, the students will participate in a small tournament.

English and Mixed Media

In our English and Mixed Media course, students have 3 hours of English in the morning.
After lunch, the students will learn how to take photos, make videos and edit them.
At the end of the week, the students will present their photos and videos.
